about

Hi, my name is Satoshi. I am a recent graduate from the Computing Department at Ara Institute of Canterbury. I was graded above A in most courses at Ara. Specifically, I achieved A+ grades in all my programming courses. I am capable of front-end programming using HTML5/CSS3, JavaScript ES6, and frameworks such as Angular and Bootstrap.

My capstone industry project at Ara was developing quiz programs in plain JavaScript. There were badly coded, malfunctioning original programs and I refactored them applying MVC architecture and added a lot of new features. Unit testing was automated using Jasmine framework. The project was successfully managed applying a Scrum approach.

Before studying at Ara, I worked as a systems architect at NTT Software Corporation in Japan. Having been a systems architect, I decided to learn programming because I had some chances to do programming and I quite liked it.

My long term goal is to be a full-stack engineer as I'm interested in business logics behind the scenes.
